About CourioSys

Smart, reliable and efficient courier management system

Who We Are

CourioSys is a courier management system designed to simplify parcel booking, tracking, and delivery operations. It connects customers, staff, and administrators on a single platform.

Our system ensures smooth parcel flow from sender to receiver with real-time updates, efficient staff assignment, and accurate tracking.

Key Features

Parcel Booking

Easy booking with sender and receiver details.

Real-time Tracking

Track parcel status at every stage.

Auto Staff Assignment

System assigns staff based on branch.

Branch Management

Organized handling of parcels via branches.

Secure Delivery

Safe and reliable parcel handling.
